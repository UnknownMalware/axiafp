What is the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus?

There are actually two Fundamental Theorems of Calculus, both of which establish a relationship between functions, their derivatives, and their antiderivatives. The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus tells us states the relationship between the rate of change of the area under a function, (the integral), and the function itself.
